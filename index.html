    Welcome to the page for Fighting Game.

    This project was done as a way to reacquaint myself with C++ after having not used it in about a year and as a way of testing out the new SFML 2.1.

    The project has gone through a number of revisions and was originally planned to be a large game with a full release, but my inability to make graphics and the discovery of an even better game idea turned this into a proof of concept.

    It consists of two fighters , one on the left and one on the right and the first to five hits loses. Gameplay is similar to Street Fighter, but the attacks are limited to Light, Medium and Heavy and can be performed when standing and when crouching.

    Overall it was a very educational experience and I learned concepts that I am bringing over to my next big project.
